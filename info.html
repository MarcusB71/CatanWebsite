History

1995

Game of the Year

The first year of its release in Germany, Catan earned awards for best rules and best game of the year.

1996

Settler of Catan

Settlers of Catan is released in English to be sold in the United States.

1997

Card Game + Expansion

Catan sold over 1.4 million copies of their card game and introduced their first expansion called Seafarers which sold over 1 million copies.

2010

Benjamin Teuber

Bejamin Teuber joins CATAN GmbH as managing director

2018

Catan World Championship

Participants from 46 nations competed to be called the best Catan player in the world.

2022

Catan Anniversary

Marks the 25 year anniversary of Catan. The game has sold over 35 million copies worldwide in 40 different languages.

Resources

  • Lumber

Special Rules

7 Roll

  • Any player with 7+ resource cards loses half of them
  • Player can activate Robber, deactivating a hex, and stealing 1 resource from nearby player

Trade-In's

  • player with a building on the port gains that trade in
  • any player can trade-in 4:1

Tips and Strategies

Strategies:

Starting Position

Place your first pieces in a position that not only has common dice roll numbers, but one that enables a strategy. For example, you might place your first piece in a position that is surrounded by lumber with easy access to a lumbar trade-in port

Expand Quickly

Gaining as much territory as possible early can give you access to more resources while cutting off your opponents.

Diversify Resources

Having access to more resources means that you arent going to have to take inefficient trade-ins and wont be reliant on trading with opponents

Development Cards

Development cards can be extremely useful especially when your current resources would allow you to purchase one, while being short for other things. They also allow you to make unpredictable moves.

Negotiation

Negotiating is one of the best ways of getting ahead of your opponents as others might be short a resource which you have an abundance of. If your opponents need something critically, be sure to leverage this in a trade.

Ports

Having access to ports is one of the best ways you can leverage an abundant resource. Having a 2:1 trade in on Lumber is great if you are getting a ton of lumber.

End Game

Be aware of how many points each player has and their win condition or else the end of the game might come sooner than you expect.
