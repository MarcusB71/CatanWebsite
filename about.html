About the Author

Marcus Brown

I am a student at Seneca who enjoys getting immersed in programming. I chose Catan for my website as it is a game I always enjoyed playing with my friends and it has a great theme to it. The level of competitiveness that the game brings out is serious enough that the trade negotiations can be intense, but it never becomes too serious.
